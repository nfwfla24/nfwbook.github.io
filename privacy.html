Privacy Policy

Last updated:

New Financial World ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, purchase resources, or engage our consulting and educational services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide (e.g., name, email address) when you email us or book a session.
  • Booking details submitted via scheduling tools (e.g., date/time selections, notes).
  • Transactional information related to purchases handled by third party payment processors (see Section 6).
  • Technical data such as IP address, device and browser type, and pages visited, which may be collected via cookies or similar technologies.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide consulting and educational services.
  • To process orders and deliver digital resources.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve our website and user experience.
  • To communicate updates, administrative messages, or policy changes.

3. Legal Basis

We process information as necessary to perform a contract, with your consent, and/or for our legitimate interests (e.g., site security and service improvement), in compliance with applicable law.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who assist us in operating the website, booking sessions, delivering resources, or processing payments. These providers are authorized to use information only as necessary to provide services to us.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies for essential functions (e.g., session management) and to understand aggregate usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Payments and Transactions

We do not collect or store payment card or wallet private key information on this site. Purchases may be processed by third party providers (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Coinbase Commerce, or your crypto wallet). Your transactions are subject to each provider’s own privacy policy and terms.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your information. To exercise these rights, contact us at nfwfla@gmail.com.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Users Under 18

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years old.

11. International Transfers

Information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure an adequate level of protection consistent with applicable law.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest revision. Material changes will be communicated through the website.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, contact us at nfwfla@gmail.com or schedule time via our calendar: Open My Calendar.
