Architecture Overview

Two layers. One verifiable chain of custody.

BRIS operates as the private intelligence layer — scoring asset reliability from sensor and maintenance data, producing a canonical record. VRL operates above it as the public trust layer — taking only a hash and opaque reference from any upstream source and anchoring it to a public chain.

BRIS — Private. Proprietary. The Layer of Truth.
VRL — Public anchor. Producer-agnostic. The Trust Layer.
