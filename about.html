About The New Financial World LLC.

The New Financial World LLC is an institutional advisory and media platform focused on energy reliability attestation, distributed-ledger methodology, and long-horizon infrastructure change.

Institutional profile

What The New Financial World LLC is built to examine.

The New Financial World LLC examines how energy infrastructure, verifiable systems, and institutional trust are becoming more closely connected. Its work is intended to support clearer thinking around reliability, attestation, and the changing relationship between physical infrastructure and digital systems.

Three pillars

The operating frame behind the firm’s work.

Pillar one

Energy reliability attestation

The firm’s work is grounded in the question of how reliability can be understood, documented, and supported in critical infrastructure environments where continuity and confidence matter.

Pillar two

Distributed-ledger methodology

NFW approaches distributed-ledger systems as institutional methodology: a way to think about records, verification, and attestation in settings where trust must be structured carefully.

Pillar three

Institutional advisory

The advisory side of the firm is oriented toward utilities, regulators, institutional finance teams, and strategic counterparts working through infrastructure and verification questions.

Founder

BRIS and VRL originate in the founder’s methodology work.

Zakir Sadiki
Founder, The New Financial World LLC

Zakir Sadiki is the founder of The New Financial World LLC and the inventor of BRIS, the Baseline Reliability Intelligence System, and VRL, the Verified Reliability Layer.

His work focuses on bringing verifiable reliability attestation and distributed-ledger methodology to energy infrastructure, helping utilities, regulators, and institutional finance teams establish a clearer layer of truth and trust around critical infrastructure reliability.
