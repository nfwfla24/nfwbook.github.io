Founder

Zakir Sadiki

Zakir Sadiki founded The New Financial World LLC to help bridge the gap between traditional infrastructure thinking and emerging digital systems. His work focuses on translating developments in blockchain, energy, and financial infrastructure into clear, usable insight for a broader audience.

Through research, writing, media, and advisory work, he helps others understand not just what is changing, but why it matters. The emphasis is on long-term shifts, real-world systems, and practical understanding rather than speculation.
