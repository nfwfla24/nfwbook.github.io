What NFW Is

Not a crypto company. An infrastructure intelligence company.

The New Financial World LLC exists to help people and organizations understand the structural shifts reshaping modern infrastructure — and to develop the methodologies that make those systems more verifiable, defensible, and trustworthy.

As energy systems become more dependent on real-time intelligence, and as financial and regulatory systems demand higher-quality evidence from those systems, the boundaries between physical infrastructure, digital rails, and capital markets are converging.

NFW works at that boundary — developing the methodologies (BRIS and VRL), advisory frameworks, and educational materials that translate that convergence into practical, institutional-grade insight.

Founder

Built on systems thinking from the inside out

Zakir Sadiki founded The New Financial World LLC with a specific thesis: that energy infrastructure reliability can be made verifiable in a way that withstands legal, regulatory, and financial scrutiny — and that doing so requires building the right methodologies from the ground up, not adapting tools built for other industries.

Working from inside the energy sector — as an internal champion rather than an external vendor — he developed BRIS and VRL as distinct methodologies: one for scoring and storing private reliability intelligence, one for anchoring that intelligence publicly in a tamper-evident, chain-agnostic way.

His approach to advisory, research, and media follows the same principle: translate complexity into precision, not simplification. The emphasis is always on long-term infrastructure shifts, real-world systems, and institutional-grade clarity.

Areas of Focus

Where our work lives

Energy Infrastructure

Understanding the physical and operational systems that underpin grid reliability — generators, turbines, transformers, substations — and how their health data flows into compliance and capital decisions.

Distributed Attestation

Blockchain and distributed ledger technology as infrastructure for verifiable evidence — not speculation. How tamper-evident timestamps, hash anchors, and co-endorsement create a verifiable chain of custody.

Regulatory Compliance

NERC/CIP standards, FEMA frameworks, and the evidentiary requirements of grid reliability proceedings — how verifiable data systems can make compliance less burdensome and more defensible.

Financial Infrastructure

How energy reliability data intersects with capital markets — asset valuation, insurance underwriting, RECs and carbon credits, and the emerging role of verifiable on-chain evidence in risk pricing.

Digital Finance Education

Cryptocurrency, Web3, DeFi, and blockchain infrastructure explained for a non-speculative audience — the foundational text and media content that forms the public-facing educational arm of NFW.

Institutional Adoption

The path from internal demo to institutional adoption — how a working testnet and clear methodology narrative becomes the basis for grant funding, pilot agreements, and multi-stakeholder buy-in.
