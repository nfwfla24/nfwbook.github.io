Crypto: Why Regulatory Clarity Matters

For years, digital asset companies have operated under overlapping and often conflicting regulatory frameworks.

The CLARITY Act aims to establish clearer boundaries between securities regulation, commodity regulation, and payment assets.

While legislation rarely generates headlines, clear rules may ultimately prove more important than market cycles.

Potential Impact

✓ Greater regulatory certainty

✓ Easier institutional participation

✓ Reduced compliance risk

✓ Clearer stablecoin frameworks

✓ Faster tokenization adoption

Infrastructure scales faster when participants understand the rules.

Continuous Money Is No Longer A Theory

For more than a century, payroll has operated in batches.

Employees work. Payroll is processed. Funds settle days later.

Zebec is challenging that model through streaming payments that move value continuously rather than waiting for scheduled pay cycles.

Through its partnership with Stellar, Zebec is expanding stablecoin payroll infrastructure designed for real-world businesses and global workforces.
