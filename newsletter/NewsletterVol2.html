Crypto: CLARITY Act Advances

The CLARITY Act (H.R. 3633) is the first major U.S. law defining which crypto assets are commodities (CFTC) vs. securities (SEC). House passed it July 2025; Senate vote expected mid-2026 [web:24][web:28].

The act creates a "mature blockchain test" allowing tokens to move from SEC to CFTC oversight once sufficiently decentralized. Truly decentralized protocols may be largely exempt [web:28].

"For US crypto businesses, the CLARITY Act promises clear registration paths, legal cover for DeFi developers, and reduced risk for banks offering custody." [web:24]
