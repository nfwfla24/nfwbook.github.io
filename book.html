Overview

What this book covers.

This book is designed as a broad introduction to blockchain rather than a narrow technical manual or trading guide. It is intended to help readers understand where blockchain came from, how digital asset systems evolved, how regulation shapes the space, and where these systems may be heading next.

Contents

The main themes addressed in the book.

Theme one

History and foundations

The book introduces blockchain through its origins, early development, and the broader shift toward digital systems of record, trust, and coordination.

Theme two

Regulatory context

It addresses how regulation influences blockchain and digital asset systems, including the institutional questions that arise when new technologies begin to intersect with public oversight.

Theme three

Future direction

The book looks ahead to how blockchain-related systems may continue to evolve and how they may shape broader conversations about digital trust, coordination, and long-term infrastructure change.
